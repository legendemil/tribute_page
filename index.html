Barney Stinson

A man who makes you happy!

When I get sad, I stop being sad and be awesome instead.
  • 1974 - Born in Staten Island.
  • 1998 - Had graduated college.
  • 2001 - Met Ted Mosby in the men's bathroom at MacLaren's.
  • Episode Sweet Taste of Liberty - Barney gets Ted to stop their usual routine of going to MacLaren's Pub and instead go to an airport to meet women at baggage claim.
  • Episode Cupcake - Barney tricks Marshall into buying an extremely expensive suit that he can't afford so he'll come work with him at his company.
  • Season 4 - Barney realizes that he has "caught" feelings for Robin and comes to Lily for advice.
  • 2009 - Marshall and Barney come up with a complicated ruse to trick Ted after the two of them learn that his project of creating a new GNB headquarters has been canceled.
  • Episode Ducky TieBarney makes a bet with Marshall that he can do all the Shinjitsu techniques, in exchange for touching Lily's boobs.
  • Episode The Slutty Pumpkin Returns - It was revealed that Barney is one quarter Canadian. Barney was horrified and Robin went out of her way to emphasize Barney's Canadian heritage to compensate for all the mockery she has endured from Barney over the years.
  • 2012 - Barney proposes to Robin, which she accepts.
  • 2013 - Barney and Robin got married.
  • 2017 - Barney stops smoking after his divorce to Robin.
